## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Go to Google Business Profile
Visit **business.google.com** and sign in with the Google account you want to manage the listing from (use contact@clinicflowautomation.com if possible).

### 2. Add your business
- Click **"Add your business to Google"**
- Business name: **ClinicFlow Automation**
- Category: Search for **"Marketing Agency"** or **"Software Company"** — choose whichever Google accepts

### 3. Set service area
- ClinicFlow is a service-area business (no physical storefront clients visit)
- Select **"No, I don't want to add a location customers can visit"**
- Service area: **Canada** (add all provinces)
