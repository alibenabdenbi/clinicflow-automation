On average, 20–30% of inbound calls during busy periods go unanswered. Most callers don't leave a voicemail — they call the clinic down the street.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does setup take?
We handle everything. Most clinics are live within 5 business days of confirming their package. You don't need to install anything or learn new software.
Do we need new software?
No. We build on top of what you already use — your existing email, phone line, and booking system. Nothing gets ripped out or replaced.
What if we already have some automation?
We audit what you have and fill the gaps. If your current system handles reminders but not recall or missed calls, we build around it — not over it.
Is there a monthly fee?
No monthly fees. One-time setup. Optional support retainer available if you want ongoing adjustments, but most clinics don't need it.
Is my patient data safe?
Yes. We never store patient data. Automations use only the information you provide — clinic name, phone, booking link. Any patient list used for reactivation is deleted within 7 days of campaign completion. Everything complies with PIPEDA (Canada's privacy law).
What if I already have appointment reminders through my booking software?
Keep them. Our system adds the layers your booking software doesn't cover: missed call recovery, patient reactivation, and Google review generation. No conflict with Jane App, Dentrix, Curve, or any PMS.
Do I need to sign a contract?
No contracts, ever. One-time payment, you own everything we set up. There's no lock-in and no cancellation process — because there's nothing to cancel.
What happens if I need changes after delivery?
All packages include 30 days of adjustments at no extra charge. After that, changes are available at $75/hour — or add our $97/month support retainer, which includes up to 2 sequence updates per month and one new automation per quarter.
How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?
Agencies charge $1,500–$5,000/month and focus on acquiring new patients. We focus on keeping and recovering the patients you already have — it's faster, cheaper, and more predictable. Missed call recovery and reactivation have direct, measurable revenue impact within weeks.
Do you work with Jane App?
Yes. Our automations work alongside Jane App — we handle the follow-up communication, Jane handles the bookings. No conflict, no replacement. This is also true for Dentrix, Curve, PowerDent, and any other PMS your clinic uses.
What if it doesn't work for us?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all packages. If the system isn't working as described within 30 days of go-live, we'll fix it or refund you — no questions asked.
What do you actually build for me?
We set up your patient reactivation campaign directly, connect your appointment reminder system, and configure your missed call follow-up. You don't touch anything technical — we build it, you approve it, and it runs.
How does the split payment work?
You send the first half before we start. We build everything within 48 hours. Once you confirm it's working, you send the second half. You never pay the full amount until you've seen it live.
What do I need to provide?
Your patient list as a CSV export from your booking system, your booking link, and your Google review link. We handle everything else.
How long until patients start receiving emails?
Within 48 hours of receiving your patient list.
