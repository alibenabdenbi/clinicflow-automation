How many calls does a dental clinic miss?

Research across Canadian dental practices shows that the average clinic misses between 15% and 30% of incoming calls. During peak hours — typically 12pm to 2pm when front desk staff are occupied with in-person patients — missed call rates can climb to 40% or higher.

Industry benchmark: A dental clinic receiving 25 calls per day and missing 20% loses approximately 5 patient opportunities daily. At an average visit value of $250, that's $1,250 per day — or over $27,000 per month in potential revenue walking out the door.

Why do dental clinics miss so many calls?

The most common reasons dental offices miss patient calls:

  • Front desk staff occupied with in-person patients during appointments
  • Lunch hour coverage gaps (12pm–2pm is peak missed call time)
  • After-hours calls with no automated follow-up system in place
  • High call volume during appointment reminder periods (Monday mornings)
  • Single-staff offices during busy chair time
  • Hold times too long — patients hang up and call the next clinic on Google

What happens when a patient can't reach your clinic?

Studies show that 70% of patients who reach voicemail or get no answer do not leave a message. They search for the next available clinic and book there. A missed call is almost never a delayed call — it's a lost patient.

For a patient searching "dentist near me," your clinic competes against every other clinic within a few kilometres. The one that responds first wins the appointment. Response time — measured in minutes, not hours — determines who gets the booking.

How do high-rated dental clinics handle missed calls?

Clinics with 4.8+ star ratings consistently show fewer "couldn't reach them" complaints in Google reviews. The pattern is clear: they respond to missed calls within minutes. The most effective approach is automatic SMS follow-up — when a patient calls and no one answers, they receive a personalized text within 60 seconds. This keeps the patient engaged before they search for alternatives.

How is missed call revenue loss calculated?

The formula used in this calculator:

  • Missed calls per month = Daily calls × Missed rate % × Working days
  • Patients lost = Missed calls × 70% (industry hang-up rate)
  • Revenue lost = Patients lost × Average visit value
  • Recoverable revenue = Revenue lost × 60% (ClinicFlow recovery rate)

The 70% hang-up rate and 60% recovery rate are conservative estimates based on dental practice communication research and client data from active ClinicFlow installations.
