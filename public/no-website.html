FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know anything about websites or technology?
No. We handle everything technical. Your only job is to answer a few questions about your clinic in a 30-minute call, review the draft site, and approve. We take care of the rest — including hosting, domain setup, and automation wiring.
How long until the site is live?
5 business days from your kickoff call. Most clinics are online within 3–4 days if the review round goes smoothly. We've never missed the 5-day window.
What if I already have a Google Business Profile?
Even better — we optimize what you already have rather than starting from scratch. Category selection, photo guidelines, review strategy, and linking it correctly to your new website all make a significant difference in local search rankings.
What phone system do I need for the missed call automation?
Most clinic phone systems are compatible. We set up the automation on Twilio (a Canadian-compliant VoIP layer) which routes alongside your existing number. You keep your current phone number — no changes to how your front desk works.
Is patient data safe?
Yes. All automations comply with PIPEDA (Canada's federal privacy law). Patient data is never stored by ClinicFlow and never shared with third parties. The only data that flows is what's needed to trigger a reminder or reply — name, phone/email, and appointment time.
Can I update the website myself after setup?
Yes. We build on a CMS that lets you update text and photos without any coding. If you'd rather not deal with it, the Full plan includes 6 months of updates done for you.
What's the difference between this and a marketing agency?
Agencies typically charge $500–$2,000/month with no defined end. ClinicFlow Digital is a one-time setup — you pay once and own everything. We're not managing ongoing campaigns; we're building the foundation and the automation layer that runs without ongoing fees.
