Common questions

What if patients realize it's automated?
In practice they don't — responses are personalized to your clinic's voice. But even if they do, an automatic reply in 60 seconds is far better than voicemail or silence.

Any practice management software — Dentrix, Dolphin, Jane, or a simple spreadsheet. We just need a patient list export. Most clinics do this in under 5 minutes.
What happens after 30 days?
