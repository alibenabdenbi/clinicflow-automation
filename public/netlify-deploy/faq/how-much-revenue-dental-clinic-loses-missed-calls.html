How much revenue does a dental clinic lose from missed calls?

At an average Canadian dental visit value of $250 and a 20% missed call rate, a clinic receiving 25 calls per day loses approximately $14,000-$27,500 per month in potential revenue. The calculation: 25 calls x 20% missed x 70% who don't call back x 22 working days x $250 per visit.
