Common questions

Is it really free forever?
Yes. Beta partners never pay. Not now, not in 6 months, not ever. The only ask is a testimonial and case study after 60 days of genuine use.
What if I don't like it after 60 days?
You cancel the call forwarding setting on your phone and the system stops. No contract, no cancellation fee. We'd ask for honest feedback on what didn't work — that's all.
What does "case study" mean exactly?
A short page on our website showing your clinic name, city, and real numbers — how many missed calls were recovered, how many patients booked via SMS, impact on no-shows. You review it before we publish anything.
Do I need to change my phone system?
No. You enable one call forwarding setting on your existing phone — takes 2 minutes. We walk you through it step by step.
Why only 5 clinics?
We want to give each beta partner proper attention. Ali personally manages every setup and is the direct contact for all 5 clinics during the beta period. More than 5 would mean less attention for each.
