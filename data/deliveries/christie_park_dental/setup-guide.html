5 min Office Manager

Create Your Mailchimp Account

WHERE TO CLICK
mailchimp.com homepage → large blue 'Sign Up Free' button, top right
  1. Go to mailchimp.com and click Sign Up Free
  2. Use your clinic email as the account email
  3. Enter Christie Park Dental as your organization name
  4. Select the Free plan — supports 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month
  5. Check your inbox and click the verification link
TIP
No credit card required. The free plan is enough to start all your automations.
10 min Office Manager

Import Your Patient List

WHERE TO CLICK
Mailchimp → Audience (left sidebar) → Add contacts → Import contacts → Upload a CSV
  1. Export your patient list from your practice management software (ask your vendor if unsure how)
  2. Your export should include: First Name, Last Name, Email, Last Visit Date
  3. In Mailchimp: click Audience → Add contacts → Import contacts
  4. Upload your CSV file and map the columns when prompted
  5. Click Import — Mailchimp will confirm how many contacts were added
TIP
Re-import monthly to keep your list current. Mailchimp handles duplicates automatically.
30 min Office Manager

Set Up Missed Call Recovery

WHERE TO CLICK
Mailchimp → Automations → Create → Classic Automations → Welcome new subscribers
  1. In Mailchimp: Automations → Create → Classic Automations
  2. Rename it: Missed Call Recovery — Christie Park Dental
  3. Click Edit trigger → change to API / Zapier
  4. Add Email 1: delay = Immediately → paste from missed-call-followup.html, Email 1
  5. Add Email 2: delay = 2 hours → paste Email 2 content
  6. Add Email 3: delay = 1 day → paste Email 3 content
  7. Click Next → Start Sending
TIP
Open missed-call-followup.html in a second tab — each email has a Copy button.
20 min Office Manager

Set Up Appointment Reminders

WHERE TO CLICK
Mailchimp → Automations → Create → Classic Automation → Date-based
  1. In Mailchimp: Automations → Create → Classic Automation → Date-based
  2. Name it: Appointment Reminders
  3. Set trigger to your appointment date field
  4. Add Email at 72 hours before → paste from appointment-reminder.html
  5. Add Email at 24 hours before → paste 24h content
  6. For SMS: use SimpleTexting or EZTexting ($25–40/month) with the SMS templates provided
TIP
SMS reminders have 98% open rates vs 20% for email. Worth the $25/month for high-value patients.
30 min Office Manager

Set Up New Patient Welcome

WHERE TO CLICK
Mailchimp → Audience → Tags → Create tag 'New Patient' → Automations → trigger: tag added
  1. Create a New Patient tag in your Mailchimp Audience
  2. In Mailchimp: Automations → Create → Classic Automation
  3. Trigger: Tag added to contact → select 'New Patient'
  4. Add 4 emails from new-patient-welcome.html:
  5.   Email 1: Immediately  |  Email 2: 1 day  |  Email 3: 2 days  |  Email 4: 8 days
  6. Replace [ReviewLink] in Email 4 with your Google Business Profile review URL
TIP
Get your Google Review link: search your clinic on Google Maps → your listing → 'Write a review' → copy the URL.
30 min setup · runs monthly Office Manager

Set Up Reactivation Campaign

WHERE TO CLICK
Mailchimp → Audience → Import contacts → tag as 'Reactivation-[Month]' → Automations → tag trigger
  1. Export inactive patients from your PMS (last visit > 12 months ago)
  2. Import them into Mailchimp and tag as Reactivation-[Month]
  3. In Mailchimp: Automations → Create → trigger: tag added
  4. Add 5 emails from reactivation-campaign.html:
  5.   Email 1: Immediately | Email 2: 14 days | Email 3: 28 days | Email 4: 42 days | Email 5: 56 days
  6. Repeat this monthly with a fresh export of inactive patients
TIP
Focus the first batch on patients inactive 12–18 months. They have the highest rebook rate (15–25%).
15 min Office Manager

Set Up Review Requests

WHERE TO CLICK
Mailchimp → Automations → Create → Date-based → appointment completion date field
  1. In Mailchimp: Automations → Create → Date-based
  2. Trigger: 2 days after appointment completion date
  3. Add Email 1 from review-request.html (Primary Message)
  4. Add Email 2: 5 days after Email 1 (Follow-Up)
  5. Replace [ReviewLink] with your Google Business Profile review URL
  6. Test the link in a private browser window — it should open the review box directly
TIP
A steady stream of reviews is one of the highest-ROI activities for a dental clinic. This runs on autopilot.
