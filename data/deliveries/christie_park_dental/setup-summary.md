## What Was Built

Your Growth package includes 7 automation systems:

| # | Automation | What it does |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Missed Call Recovery** | Texts and emails patients within 2 minutes of a missed call — 3-message sequence |

| 4 | **Reactivation Campaign** | 5-email sequence for patients not seen in 12+ months — runs monthly |
| 5 | **Review Requests** | Automated Google review ask, 2 days after each completed visit |
| 6 | **Monthly Newsletter** | Template-based monthly patient email — fill in 2 fields, we handle the send |
| 7 | **Tracking Dashboard** | Google Sheets tracker for all key metrics |

---

## How the Automations Fire

None of these require front desk action to trigger. Each fires based on an event:

- **Missed call** → triggers the 3-message missed call recovery sequence
- **New appointment booked** → triggers appointment reminders at 72h and 24h
- **New patient books first appointment** → triggers the welcome sequence *(Growth/Full)*
- **Appointment marked completed** → triggers the review request *(Growth/Full)*
- **You send us the inactive patient list** → triggers the reactivation campaign *(Growth/Full)*

Your front desk's only ongoing task: handle replies from patients (these come to your regular inbox), and send the monthly reactivation list.
