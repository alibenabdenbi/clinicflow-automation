# Milestones & Payment Plan — Client project

**Payment structure:** Deposit + milestone billing (recommended)

## Milestone 1 — Kickoff + first deliverables
**%:** 40%  
**Due:** Day 0–4 (within 7–14 days (estimate))  
**Includes:**
- Discovery call + requirements summary
- Scope document + timeline

## Milestone 2 — Core build
**%:** 30%  
**Due:** Midpoint (within 7–14 days (estimate))  
**Includes:**
- Deliverable #1
- Deliverable #2

## Milestone 3 — Final + handoff
**%:** 30%  
**Due:** Final day (within 7–14 days (estimate))  
**Includes:**
- Final fixes + handoff


## Payment Terms
- Invoice due: **Net 7** (7 days)
- Late fee: **2% per week** after due date (optional)
- Work pauses if payment is overdue by 7+ days
